WHAT IS NO LIMIT?

Who we are

About Us

No Limit is a set of boutique fitness studios in New York state that offers group fitness classes, our original concept HIIT class, Cycle, Barre, Row, and Yoga.
In addition to group classes, we have an always open "No Limit Lab" that allows you to conduct individual training, on your own, or with a personal trainer.
No Limit is NOT your average gym.
No Limit was born from a desire to start a unique studio experience that we would be proud to call our own.
No Limit is locally owned and operated and is a labor of love from a small team of passionate individuals.
Regardless of your age or physical fitness level, No Limit is an all-inclusive group fitness studio that offers a variety of group and individual level programming to meet your needs.
Our approach ensures we continue to deliver a high-quality experience for you day after day.
We want you to enjoy No Limit as much as we enjoy having you in our studio.

The trainers

Beth Nicely

Founder


Founder of The Limit, Beth is a NASM Certified Personal Trainer and PROnatal Pre/Postnatal Specialist.

In addition to 9 years of personal training experience, Beth has been a professional dancer for 18 years. She was a Radio City Rockette and has been in 6 Broadway shows. She was most recently on the choreography team for Netflix's "The Prom". She started dancing at three years old and grew up training and competing in tap, jazz, and ballet.

Trainer
